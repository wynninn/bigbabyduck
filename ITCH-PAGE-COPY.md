## Description (main body)

**You are a big baby duck on a mission: eat 1000 watermelons.**

Waddle through 15 hand-built worlds — savanna, deep space, spooky forest, underwater ocean, panda bamboo grove, dino valley (watch out for the T-Rex), winterland, a whole carnival Funland, a kart-racing speedway, your own farm, and more.

🦆 Every melon you eat hatches a **duckling** that follows you — build a parade of 30
🍈 Every 10 melons, **Melon Bob** pops in to help (he's a giant walking watermelon, don't ask)
🏎️ Race world champions in karts, planes and hot-air balloons
🎮 Funland: bowling, trampolines, floor piano, whack-a-mole, dominoes, fireworks
🏠 Enter houses in the city — six themed interiors with secrets inside
👒 Hats, paint and stickers for you AND your ducklings
⏱️ **Speedrun timer** with saved best times — how fast can you hit 1000?

**Two players, one keyboard** — split-screen mode where Player 2 waddles with WASD. Perfect for playing with your kid (or your chat).

No downloads. No accounts. No ads. Just duck.

*Press P for photo mode 📸 — share your flock!*
